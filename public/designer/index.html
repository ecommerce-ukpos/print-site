Adobe Express Free account required

Browser-based upscaler from Adobe — no software to install. Upload your image, click upscale, download. The most recognisable name here and very straightforward to use.

✓ Pros

  • No software to install
  • Very easy — works in your browser
  • Trusted Adobe brand

✗ Cons

  • Requires a free Adobe account
  • Limited free exports per month
  • Quality average vs. dedicated tools

Upscayl 100% Free

Free, open-source desktop app. Runs AI upscaling entirely on your own computer — ideal for confidential client artwork.

✓ Pros

  • Completely free, no account needed
  • Artwork stays on your machine
  • Batch processing supported

✗ Cons

  • Requires a one-time download & install
  • Slower without a dedicated GPU
  • Desktop only
